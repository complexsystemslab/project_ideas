# 🚀 Fully Funded PhD Studentship in AI – University of York, UK

**Are you passionate about the future of Artificial Intelligence? Looking for the freedom to pursue groundbreaking ideas on broad generalization, reasoning, and safety?**

We are seeking a motivated, curious, and independent PhD student to join our research group at the **University of York**. This is a fully funded position open to **UK Home students (EU students are also highly welcome to apply).**

### 🧠 Complete Intellectual Freedom (With High-Impact Projects)

The best part about this studentship? **You shape your own journey.** You will have complete freedom to define your research direction within the broader AI landscape—from core methods to applications, and from theory to systems.

To give you an idea of the cutting-edge directions we are actively exploring, you will have the opportunity to dive deep into major projects like:

* **Infusing Reasoning into LLMs (The ARC Challenge):** It has been widely suggested that large language models cannot truly reason. We are changing that. This project involves infusing reasoning and priors into LLMs, LLMs/LVMs, and multimodal systems, applying them to François Chollet's **Abstraction and Reasoning Corpus (ARC)**.
* **Generative & Intelligent Agents:** Building advanced, autonomous agents to solve the ultra-ambitious **ARC-AGI 3.0 challenge**, as well as leveraging databases of human problem-solving to augment human performance with LLMs.
* **AI Safety & The Bigger Questions:** Tackling robust AI safety frameworks, evaluating ideological encoding, and addressing vital questions around AI and society.
